1️⃣ Total Main Panels (Typical Structure)

  • Usually 5–6 main panels hote hain.

1. Super Admin Panel

  • Ye company (PagarBook) ke internal staff use karte hain.
  • Functions:
  • All businesses manage
  • Subscription plans
  • Payment monitoring
  • User KYC verification
  • Support tickets
  • Analytics dashboard
  • App settings
  • Notification management
  • Approx Screens: 20–25

2. Business Owner / Admin Panel

  • Ye shop owner / company owner use karta hai.
  • Functions:
  • Staff add / remove
  • Attendance track
  • Salary calculate
  • Advance payment
  • Leave management
  • Reports
  • Export data
  • WhatsApp / SMS salary slip
  • Approx Screens: 15–20

3. Employee Panel (Mobile App)

  • Employees apni details check karte hain.
  • Functions:
  • Attendance mark
  • Salary details
  • Leave apply
  • Profile update
  • Notifications
  • Work hours
  • Approx Screens: 8–10

4. Authentication Panel

  • Login / Signup / OTP verification system.
  • Functions:
  • Mobile OTP login
  • Business registration
  • Password reset
  • Invite employee
  • Approx Screens: 4–5

5. Payment / Subscription Panel

  • Paid features manage karta hai.
  • Functions:
  • Plan selection
  • Payment gateway
  • Invoice
  • Renewal
  • Trial activation
  • Approx Screens: 4–6

2️⃣ Complete Workflow (Step by Step)

Step 1 — Business Signup

  • Owner:
  • Mobile Number

↓

  • OTP Verification

↓

  • Business Name

↓

  • Create Account

Step 2 — Staff Add

  • Admin panel:
  • Add Employee

↓

  • Name + Mobile

↓

  • Salary Type (Monthly / Daily)

↓

  • Invite Send
  • Employee ko SMS invite milta hai.

Step 3 — Attendance System

  • Employee:
  • Login

↓

  • Mark Attendance

↓

  • Present / Absent / Half Day

↓

  • Time In / Time Out
  • Admin panel me attendance reflect ho jata hai.

Step 4 — Salary Calculation

  • System automatically calculate karta hai:
  • Monthly Salary

- Absent Days

+ Overtime

+ Advance Adjust

= Net Salary

Step 5 — Salary Slip

  • Admin:
  • Generate Salary

↓

  • Salary Slip

↓

  • Send via WhatsApp / SMS

Step 6 — Reports

  • Owner dekh sakta hai:
  • Monthly attendance
  • Salary reports
  • Employee performance
  • Payment history
